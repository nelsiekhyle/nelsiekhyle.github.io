Explore Kyoto's Famous Tourist Spots

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates, Fushimi Inari Shrine is one of the most iconic landmarks in Kyoto.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

A beautiful and serene bamboo forest, Arashiyama is a must-visit for anyone exploring Kyoto's natural beauty.

Philosopher's Path

A picturesque walkway along a canal lined with cherry blossoms, perfect for peaceful strolls.

Nijo Castle

A UNESCO World Heritage site, Nijo Castle was built in 1603 and is an incredible example of Japanese feudal architecture.
