Why Kyoto?

Kyoto is a city on the island of Honshu, known for its classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses.

Golden Pavilion

Kinkaku-ji, or the Golden Pavilion, is a stunning temple covered in gold leaf.

Kiyomizu-dera

An iconic temple on a hill with a grand wooden stage overlooking the city.

Zen Gardens

Kyoto is home to beautiful Zen gardens, offering serenity and peace.
